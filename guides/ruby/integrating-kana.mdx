Now that you have your plans setup in Kana (with their related features) alongside all your current users, you can start to **integrate Kana with your product** in order to:

- **Subscribe** users to plans

- Record a user's **usage** of features within plans

- Identify a users **current plans**

- Identify a user's **current feature usage**
